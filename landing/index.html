Workflow

Three steps to your result

From image upload to planning — straightforward and transparent.

Step 01

Upload your image

DICOM, JPG or PNG — simply drag and drop. Full-leg AP and lateral knee views are both supported.

Step 02

Analyse the limb axis

Set anatomical landmarks guided by the app. The lower limb axis is measured and all relevant angles are calculated automatically.

Step 03

Plan & export

Plan HTO and DFO directly on the image. Export as a PDF report or PNG with all measurements included.

Who it's for

Built for orthopaedic training

CADtomie is a digital learning aid for anyone looking to understand lower limb deformities and osteotomy planning more deeply.

Residents & Medical Students

  • Draw and understand anatomical axes independently
  • Interactively explore different osteotomy types
  • Practice repeatedly with your own case examples
  • No expensive equipment required

Educators & Course Instructors

  • Prepare cases for courses and workshops
  • Work through X-rays together in real time
  • Export results as PDF for group discussion
  • Browser-based — no installation needed
