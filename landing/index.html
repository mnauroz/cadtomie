Workflow

Three steps to your result

From image upload to planning — fast and transparent.

1
🩻

Upload your image

DICOM, JPG or PNG — simply drag & drop. Full-leg AP and lateral knee views are both supported.

2
📐

Analyse the limb axis

Anatomical landmarks are set automatically and can be adjusted manually. The lower limb axis is precisely measured and visualised.

3
✂️

Plan osteotomy & export

Plan HTO and DFO directly on the image. Export results as a PDF report or PNG.

Who it's for

A training tool for orthopaedic education

CADtomie was designed as a digital learning aid — for anyone looking to deepen their understanding of lower limb deformities and osteotomy planning.

🎓 Residents & Medical Students

  • Draw and understand anatomical axes independently
  • Interactively explore different osteotomy types
  • Practice repeatedly with your own case examples
  • No expensive equipment required

🏫 Educators & Course Instructors

  • Prepare cases for courses and workshops
  • Work through X-rays together in real time
  • Export results as PDF for group discussion
  • Browser-based — no installation needed
